SpecialHaven is a community for caretakers, experts, and organizations in the disability space.

SpecialHaven is the one-stop shop for all things special needs. Caretakers, experts, and organizations all come to SpecialHaven for up-to-date news, events, podcasts, videos, and open-discussion related to disability. We aim to make the lives of all people affected easier.
